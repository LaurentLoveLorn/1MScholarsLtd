About MyAppCoach

We were built by youth who experienced missing opportunities. Now we're on a mission to make sure no one else misses theirs.

Our Story

In 2022, our founder Sarah was a brilliant student in Rwanda. She had the grades, the potential, and the dreams. But she missed a full scholarship to Harvard because she didn't know it existed. Two months later, it was announced on a obscure forum she never visited.

That experience changed everything. She realized millions of young Africans were in the same situation. Opportunities existed, but they were scattered, invisible, and confusing to access.

So we built MyAppCoach. A platform where every opportunity is centralized, every application is guided, and every young person gets a fair chance.

🎯

One missed opportunity changed our mission

We're committed to ensuring no one else experiences that same heartbreak.

Our Mission

Transform scattered opportunities into successful careers by providing African youth with centralized access, intelligent matching, expert guidance, and step-by-step application support.

Our Vision

A world where every talented young person in Africa has the tools, guidance, and community to discover and win the opportunities that change their lives.

Our Impact Goal

By 2030, help 1 million African youth successfully access and win life-changing opportunities in scholarships, jobs, and careers.
